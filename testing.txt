========================================================================
AEGIS NEXUS PRO - COMPLETE TESTING PROTOCOL
========================================================================

Follow these steps to verify that every feature of the Smart Hotspot 
Controller is functioning perfectly on your mobile hardware.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 1: THE HOSTING GATEWAY (First Android Phone)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the "Aegis Nexus Pro" app.
2. Ensure your phone's "Mobile Hotspot" is turned ON in system settings.
3. Tap the "START" button at the top right (turns red and says STOP).
4. Look at the "Gateway Setup" card. It should show an IP and Port.
   (e.g., 192.168.43.1 at port 8080)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 2: THE CLIENT BRIDGE (Second Android Phone / Laptop)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Connect the Client to your host phone's Wi-Fi.
2. Go into the Wi-Fi settings for that connection:
   - Change Proxy to "MANUAL".
   - Proxy Hostname: (The IP from the app, e.g., 192.168.43.1)
   - Proxy Port: 8080
3. Save and wait a few seconds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 3: FEATURE TEST - REAL-TIME MONITORING
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. On the Client Phone, open Chrome or YouTube and load a video.
2. On the Host Phone (the app), watch:
   - "Throughput Monitor": The blue line graph must spike to show KB/s.
   - "Usage Distribution": A new slice should appear in the Pie Chart for that client IP.
   - "Active Connections": A new tile for the client IP will appear.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 4: FEATURE TEST - SPEED RESTRICTION (THROTTLE)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start a video on the Client Phone.
2. On the Host Phone app, expand the Client's tile.
3. Tap "THROTTLE" (turns yellow/amber).
4. VERIFY: The client's video should start to buffer or slow down. 
   On the host app, look at the Throughput Monitor - it should flatten 
   out at a low speed (~125KB/s or 1MB/s).
5. Tap "UNLIMIT" to restore full speed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 5: FEATURE TEST - FIREWALL SECURITY (BLOCK)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. On the Host Phone app, tap the Blue Shield icon.
2. Type "facebook.com" and tap "Block".
3. On the Client Phone, try to open facebook.com in Chrome.
4. VERIFY: Facebook fails to load, but other sites like google.com or 
   wikipedia.com still load perfectly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 6: FEATURE TEST - DATA QUOTA (AUTO KILL-SWITCH)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. On the Host Phone app, expand the Client's tile.
2. Tap "50MB" (turns indigo).
3. Start a large download on the client.
4. VERIFY: Once the client's "Total MB" reaches 50MB, their internet 
   connection must instantly stop working.
5. Tap "RESET" to restore their connection.

========================================================================
TEST COMPLETE: If all 6 phases pass, your controller is 100% STABLE!
========================================================================
